## Hypothesis
Instead of blending TWO NS resolutions (v5: NS₂ + NS₅), blend THREE: NS₁, NS₃, NS₅. Multi-scale curvature decomposition.

## Architecture
Momentum → Triple-Resolution NS (save@1, save@3, full@5) → Three-point weighted blend → Update

## Key Formula
```
update = (1 - w₁ - w₃) * NS₅ + w₃ * scale(NS₃) + w₁ * scale(NS₁)
```

## Cost
Two extra clones (no extra matmuls). Same NS step count.

## Why It Should Work
- v5 showed save@2 >> save@3 (more curvature retained)
- save@1 has even MORE curvature (but also more noise)
- Three-point blend: NS₁ rich curvature with small weight, NS₃ stable backbone
- Multi-scale decomposition: NS₅=base, NS₃-NS₅=medium-freq, NS₁-NS₃=high-freq
