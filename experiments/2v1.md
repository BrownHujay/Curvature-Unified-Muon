**File:** `cum/cum_2v1.py`
**Status:** FAILED

## Hypothesis
Extract exact top-k singular components via randomized projection, blend with NS output. Sharper curvature signal than v5's NS₂ intermediate.

## Architecture
Momentum → NS(5) + Random Projection Top-k SVD → Blend → Update

## Cost
Extra random projection + small SVD per step. Significant compute overhead.

## Analysis
- Random projection too noisy at this scale (128×512 matrices)
- Unstable — spiked to 1.5558 at step 1999
